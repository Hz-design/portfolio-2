Plastic Whale

Scope

Cleaning the world from the plastic soup with a snowball effect.

Main job:

Prototyping

Conceptualizing

Date:

2021.3.31

Overview:

"It is a mobile phone web application with the goal of making the world plastic-free by cleaning up plastic waste. Users of the application can organize events to clean up plastic waste, earning points that they can use in stores. Besides the points, it is more enjoyable to do it with multiple people."

Key Goals:

  1. Plastic-Free World: Drive efforts to eliminate plastic waste and promote a cleaner environment.
  2. Community Engagement: Encourage users to organize cleanup events and foster a sense of collective responsibility.
  3. Rewards and Incentives: Provide points for users to redeem in stores, motivating continued participation.
  4. Environmental Awareness: Raise awareness about the impact of plastic pollution through educational content.
  5. Scalability: Design for potential expansion to make a global impact.

Tools:

  • Adobe XD

Outcome:

A mobile phone web application dedicated to achieving a plastic-free world by facilitating plastic waste cleanup and providing users with essential information about the impact of plastic waste.
