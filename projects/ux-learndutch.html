UX/UI Learn Dutch Supermarket Words

Scope

It is a web application for mobile phones. The purpose is to help international students learn Dutch supermarket words.

See Prototype

Project Case

Main job:

Research

Conceptualizing

Prototyping

Coding

Date:

2020.11.13

Overview:

The ISGA has asked the CMD students from the HVA to design a digital expansion for the booklet, which should be a functional mobile website created using html/css.

Target Audience

The target audience consists of international students aged between 18 and 30 years old who are new to Amsterdam for their studies. The duration of their studies varies from 3 months to 4 years. Additionally, this group is highly diverse, so the guide must remain neutral and suitable for everyone. With this product, international students can gain earlier access to the website, enabling them to prepare themselves in advance. During interviews, it was revealed that many issues arise with SIM cards, WiFi, transportation, and the weather, which can change rapidly.

Discovering Student Needs

Om de studenten beter te begrijpen heb ik interviews beluisterd van de internationale studenten. Uit de interviews heb ik veel nieuwe inzichten ontdekt. Hieronder heb ik een rijtje gemaakt met de 4 inzichten die mij het meest aanspraken.

Tools:

  • HTML5

  • CSS3

Outcome:

The project's outcome is an and user-friendly climbing application with responsive multi-device design and a clear, well-structured screenflow, ensuring user engagement and satisfaction.

Insights:

The Essentials, international students face challenges with using public transportation, acquiring a SIM card, and obtaining a Dutch bank card.

Map of Amsterdam, they encounter difficulty finding their way around the city due to the lack of WiFi, making it harder for them to navigate.

Food of Amsterdam, eating in Amsterdam: International students seek an affordable place to buy food, but face the challenge of all food labels being in Dutch.

If I had the booklet earlier, Essential Preparations for International Students: Detailed weather-appropriate outfit plans, an offline map of Amsterdam, and information regarding the registration day at the HVA.
