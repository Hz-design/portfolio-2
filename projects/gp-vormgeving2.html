Animated Song Text for Spotify

Scope

Perform visual research to match an atmosphere with a song lyric. Collect images that express the idea through color and shape. Use this research to design the song lyric in your unique style, aligning with the tone-of-voice of the stories.

Overview:

I selected the song "Moth to a Flame" and explored its mood by creating a mood board. Then, I proceeded to sketch the song lyrics using various materials. Afterwards, I crafted an animation based on the sketches and brought it to life!

Watch Animation

Outcome:

A lyrics animation about the song Moth to the flame. It is really experimental and there are too many styles incorporated. I'm not 100% satisfied with this piece but I did had a really fun time doing the animations!.
