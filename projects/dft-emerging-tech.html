Designing for Future Technologies: Cooking with Mixed Reality

Scope

A mixed reality prototype created in virtual reality, focusing on teaching users how to cook while reducing waste and recycling produce to be able to create a big diversity of dishes. The aim is to achieve zero waste and zero hunger, enabling anyone to cook!

Overview:

This mixed reality concept teaches consumers how to cook efficiently and make the most of their food leftovers. It is based on the principles of reducing, reusing, and recycling food waste to prevent unnecessary disposal. The target audience for this project is students, aiming to create a more sustainable generation.

Outcome:

A Virutal Reality Prototype in Unity with basic visualsations of the concepts.
